2026 LINEUP

Catch performances from Carter White, TideBreakers, Roan, and more of your favorite indie, country, and alt-rock artists lighting up the Main Stage against Vancouver’s stunning skyline.

Kicking things off on August 21, country star Carter White (11:00am) opens with heartfelt ballads and southern charm, followed by the raw energy of Indigo’s alternative rock (1:30pm). Joelle’s dreamy indie pop (3:00pm) sets the mood for the evening, leading into Denim Lace (5:30pm), whose gritty riffs close the night with nostalgic alt-rock fire.

On August 22, the soulful Tidebreakers (1:30pm) bring warmth and harmony, before Atlas (3:00pm) fills the air with lush indie-pop soundscapes. The thunderous Roan (4:30pm) ignites the stage with alt-rock power, and Hole$ (7:15pm) wrap up the day with infectious synth-driven energy.

The festival’s final day, August 23, showcases emerging country star Bloom (12:00pm), followed by veteran alt-rockers Break (2:00pm). Trinity (5:00pm) deliver roots-driven harmonies before Hippocamp (7:00pm) close the weekend with their adventurous, genre-bending country sound.
