Experience

What are we about ? At Pacific Pulse, the music is just the beginning. Dive into a three-day experience that celebrates creativity, connection, and coastal culture. From vibrant art to mouthwatering food and immersive workshops, the festival is a living, breathing community built by and for music lovers

Activity Highlights

Art workshops

Get hands-on with creative sessions led by local artists paint, sculpt, or craft your own festival keepsake.

DJ after party

When the sun sets, the Pulse keeps going. Dance under the stars to late-night sets from top DJs and surprise guests.

Art installations

Wander through immersive, ocean-inspired installations that turn Stanley Park into a living gallery of light and sound.

Jam sessions

Grab a guitar, join a drum circle, or harmonize with fellow fans in open acoustic jam spots celebrating indie and alt-rock sounds.
