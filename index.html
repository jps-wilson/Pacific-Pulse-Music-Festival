PACIFIC PULSE

August 21st - 23rd, 2026

Ceperley Meadow in Stanley Park

WHAT IS PACIFIC PULSE?

Three days. One stunning coastal setting. Endless rhythm.
Pacific Pulse brings together indie pop, alternative rock, and art in the heart of Vancouver's iconic Stanley Park.
Soak up the sunshine, dance barefoot in the grass, and celebrate the pulse of live music by the sea.
